IS SKILL FARMING STILL PROFITABLE?

This page pulls market data from EVE-Central and determines whether or not skill farming is profitable and to what degree. The setup values are set at the values most people are likely to have to start with, but can be tweaked to fit your characters. Calculations currently assume you're buying from buy orders, selling to sell orders, and are working in Jita. I'm aware that Perimeter is a high volume SP market; support for citadels might come soon.
